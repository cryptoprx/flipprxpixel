GAME OVERVIEW
-------------
FlipPRX Pixel is a modern retro-style platformer that combines classic 
platforming mechanics with innovative character abilities and intelligent 
enemy AI. Built with React and TypeScript, the game features pixel-perfect 
graphics, procedural sound generation, and smooth 60 FPS gameplay.


PLAYABLE CHARACTERS
-------------------

🎮 GUY1 - THE BALANCED ALL-ROUNDER
   • Double Jump: Perform a second jump in mid-air for extended mobility
   • Dash Ability: Press Shift to dash horizontally through obstacles
   • Particle Effects: Cyan double jump particles, orange/yellow dash trails
   • Playstyle: Versatile character perfect for beginners and advanced players
   • Best For: Players who want balanced speed and control

🏃 GUY2 - THE SPEED DEMON
   • Enhanced Air Slide: Perform up to 2 air dashes per jump
   • Speed Boost: Landing after air dash grants 2 seconds of increased speed
   • 35% Faster: Base movement speed significantly higher than other characters
   • Particle Effects: Green fart particles with sparkles and speed trails
   • Playstyle: High-speed, high-mobility character for skilled players
   • Best For: Players who love fast-paced, momentum-based gameplay

💪 GUY3 - THE POWER HOUSE
   • Charged Slam Attack: Hold jump at peak to charge (3 levels)
   • Ground Pound: Release to slam down with devastating force
   • Area Damage: Kills all enemies within impact radius
   • Brick Destruction: Breaks nearby bricks based on charge level
   • Particle Effects: Red charging particles that intensify with power
   • Playstyle: Slower but incredibly powerful, dominates with raw strength
   • Best For: Players who prefer strategic, high-impact gameplay


ENEMY TYPES & AI
----------------

🍄 GOOMBA - THE PERSISTENT PURSUER
   • Chase Behavior: Detects player within 80 pixels and pursues aggressively
   • Patrol System: Random direction changes during patrol routes
   • Speed: 35 base speed, 55 when chasing
   • Visual Feedback: Red angry particles when chasing player
   • Difficulty: Easy to Medium
   • Strategy: Keep moving to avoid being cornered by multiple goombas

🐍 SNAKE - THE LUNGING PREDATOR
   • Lunge Attack: Detects player within 60 pixels and performs jumping lunge
   • Attack Cooldown: 3-5 second cooldown between lunges
   • Warning System: Red particles appear before lunging
   • Enhanced Slithering: Variable speed based on wave motion (50 base speed)
   • Jump Height: 140 velocity for improved mobility
   • Difficulty: Medium to Hard
   • Strategy: Watch for red warning particles and time your jumps carefully

👻 BADGUY/GHOST - THE PSYCHOLOGICAL HORROR
   • Freeze Mechanic: Freezes when player stops moving
   • Stalker Mode: Slowly creeps toward player when frozen (8 speed)
   • Teleport Strategies:
     - Behind player (40% chance) - Classic horror approach
     - Above player with rush attack (30% chance) - Devastating drop
     - Ahead of player for ambush (30% chance) - Unpredictable positioning
   • Rush Attack: Drops from above at 150 speed with red warning particles
   • Dynamic Speed: 40-70 speed based on distance (faster when closer)
   • Threat Particles: Purple/magenta particles when near player
   • Jump Mimicry: Copies player jumps to stay at same height
   • Difficulty: Hard to Very Hard
   • Strategy: Keep moving constantly, never stop or the ghost will creep closer


GAME MECHANICS
--------------

MOVEMENT SYSTEM
• Smooth acceleration and deceleration
• Enhanced air control for precise platforming
• Coyote time (0.2s) for forgiving edge jumps
• Jump buffer (0.25s) for responsive input
• Variable jump height based on button hold
• Terminal velocity for controlled falling

POWER-UPS
• War Helmet: Break bricks from any angle
• Water Gun: Shoot projectiles to defeat enemies
• Coins: Collect for points and combo bonuses
• Question Blocks: Random rewards (coins, points, helmet, or enemies)

STAGE PROGRESSION
• 10 Unique Stages: Each progressively longer and more challenging
• Stage 1: 1000 pixels (Tutorial)
• Stage 10: 8200 pixels (Final Challenge)
• Dynamic Difficulty: Enemy count and placement scales with stage
• Portal System: Random blackhole mini-game with risk/reward mechanics

VISUAL EFFECTS
• Enhanced particle systems for all abilities
• Character-specific particle colors
• Screen shake effects based on impact force
• Smooth camera following with lerp
• Pixel-perfect sprite rendering
• Retro Game Boy aesthetic


TECHNICAL FEATURES
------------------

PERFORMANCE
• 60 FPS gameplay with delta time smoothing
• Optimized collision detection
• Efficient particle management
• Canvas-based rendering with image smoothing disabled

AUDIO SYSTEM
• Procedural sound generation using Web Audio API
• Background music loop (flip.mp3)
• Character-specific sound effects (bro.mp3, fart.mp3, huh.mp3)
• Dynamic audio based on game events

CONTROLS
• Arrow Keys / WASD: Movement
• Space / Z / W / Up Arrow: Jump
• Shift: Dash (Guy1 only)
• X: Shoot water gun (when equipped)
• Hold Jump: Charge abilities (Guy2 air slide, Guy3 slam)

RESPONSIVE DESIGN
• Mobile/tablet detection and optimization
• Touch controls support
• Adaptive UI scaling
• Game Boy-style presentation on mobile devices


GAMEPLAY TIPS
-------------

GENERAL STRATEGIES
1. Master the coyote time - you can jump slightly after leaving a platform
2. Use jump buffering - press jump just before landing for instant jumps
3. Collect coins in sequence for combo bonuses
4. Break bricks from below to find hidden rewards
5. Watch enemy patterns and exploit their weaknesses

CHARACTER-SPECIFIC TIPS

Guy1 (All-Rounder)
• Use double jump to reach high platforms
• Dash through tight enemy groups
• Combine dash and double jump for maximum distance
• Save dash for emergency escapes

Guy2 (Speed Demon)
• Chain air dashes for incredible horizontal distance
• Use speed boost to blitz through dangerous sections
• Hold jump while moving fast to prepare air slide
• Perfect for speedrunning stages

Guy3 (Power House)
• Charge slam to level 3 for maximum destruction
• Use ground pound to clear multiple enemies at once
• Break brick clusters to find shortcuts
• Slower speed requires more strategic positioning


COMBO SYSTEM
------------
• Defeating enemies in quick succession builds combo multiplier
• Combo timer: 3.5 seconds between kills
• Bonus points scale with combo level
• Guy1: 100 base + combo bonus
• Guy2: 150 base + combo bonus  
• Guy3: 300 base + combo bonus (ground pound)


SCORING SYSTEM
--------------
• Coins: 100 points each
• Combo Coins: 100 + (combo × 50) points
• Enemy Defeat: 100-300 points based on method
• Brick Break: 50 points
• Question Block: 500 points
• Stage Clear: Bonus based on coins collected
